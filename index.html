Features

Experts in the garment care industry.

Family Owned & Operated

What sets us apart is more than just our expertise; it's the unity of purpose that stems from being family-owned and operated. Our shared dedication infuses every interaction with warmth and a personal touch. When you trust your fabrics to Jacqui Cleaner, you're not just a customer – you're an extension of our family, and your satisfaction is our proudest achievement.

28 Years of Experience

Established since 1995, Jacqui Cleaner stands firm, upholding values that never go out of style: integrity, trust, and the time-honored traditions of a family committed to its craft. Experience the difference that a family touch can make – where impeccable service and a genuine love for fabrics come together, only at Jacqui Cleaner.

Friendly & Trustworthy

We treat our customers like family. Nestled in the heart of Humble, Texas. Jacqui Cleaner stands as a testament to the values passed down through our family's lineage. With meticulous attention to detail, we have honed our craft, perfecting techniques that not only restore your garments but also safeguard their memories. Whether it's a vintage wedding gown or a treasured everyday ensemble, we understand that each piece carries a story, and it's our privilege to play a part in its preservation.
